THINK MAGAZINE

Think is a academic project for the course of Digital Typography.
The goal was to create a digital magazine using a skilful use of the typographical and layout rules. The design process started from the redesign of a logo with the word “think”, giving a feminine and elegant style as the Milady Style. To communicate the elegance and sophistication, I used the Chapaza serif font for its beautiful contrast between the thick lines and thin lines. To recall a femmile taste I added a few sinuous elements trying to give a good balance at the logo in it’s totality.

The cover of the magazine is totally realized with the use of typography and a game of balance between a serif and a sans-serif fonts. The magazine is divided into single pages and for the layout I used Bodoni FLF in different weights and, only for a few elements I used a pink light to recall feminine and elegant style of the magazine.

SAMSUNG IOP APP

IOP is a teamworking project for the class of Service Design. Our goal was create a digital experience for Samsung to give a more personal relationship between Samsung and its customers. The concept of Internet of People means a way to create a community of users sharing their personal experiences and their emotions while interacting with Samsung. The user is invited to daily select his personal feeling between five emotional moods that will influence the way contents are displayed in the app. In the first section, shares the PURE EMOTIONS related to experiences and events promoted by Samsung IOP staff.

The second is focused on the experiences and reactions that users have while using Samsung products in their everyday life. The third section wants to build a solid relation between users and brand and itâ€™s focuses on the events that IOP selects and than proposes to users based on the mood they selected. This section has the power to create a net of real interactions between the community, inviting them to live some experience where they have the possibility to try for real Samsung products.

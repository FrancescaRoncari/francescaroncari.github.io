TRIENNALE

UGO LA PIETRA

This is a teamworking project designed for the course of Interaction Design, in IED. The request was to create media support for Triennale di Milano able to inform users in three distinct phases: before, during and after the exhibition.Starting from this point, we have studied and later designed a website that showcased an organization of contents and materials that was adaptable and applicable to all others exhibitions hosted by Triennale.

The site, designed for Ugo La Pietra’s exibition, aims to satisfy the demand of information and materials from the users before the art show, while for the phase “during” and after the art show, so we have created an application for smartphones and tablets, with a free download called “Triennale.app”, which allows users to browse also interesting extras contents.

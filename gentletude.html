GENTLETUDE IS WELLNESS

This is a teamworking thesis project designed for GENTLETUDE ONLUS. The world Gentletude imagines is a kind world to 360 ° in which people can share projects, spaces and common dreams. Our design for Gentletude ONLUS is started by asking a very important question: WHAT IS THE KINDNESS? During the first phase of the project we analyzed and discussed the concept of kindness, observing it in its many faces, in many environments and for different target. From there we started to draw a line on which we could build an intervention strategy. We set our goals, began the research phase and finally expressed our concept which is the basis of everything development work and its variations: kindness is wellness.

The project was declined by three major media / graphic tools:
       1. Restayling
       2. Manual of Kindness
       3. Gentletude Welfare Kit

2. Manual of Kindness

The Kindness Gentletude Manual is a tool which shows how easy and healthy for themselves and others, to do good. The book is as a kind of training manual for some kind sentiments. Among them there are empathy, generosity, caring and sharing. Each chapter talks of a feeling and is followed from descriptions and illustrations of four kind deeds, to do during the daily live. The gentle exercises proposed intervene and mature,the feeling in question, proposing an exercise to animals, one towards themselves, one towards the environment and, finally, one to the others.

3. Gentletude Welfare Kit

Welfare Kit Gentletude was born as an idea and project to be proposed in the environment and in the context of small-medium companies.The kit is proposed through a pack inside which, but not all, are stored various tools designed and engineered to produce welfare at work. The kit consists of:
• Brochure
• Posters
• Application
• Interactive whiteboard
• Bracelet counts beats
• Earphones
• Card with advice
• Seeds for planting
• Candles

Gentletude App

The app is designed in such a way which can work in three basic levels for business productivity and the achievement of psychological welfare in the workplace by employees.
The levels are as follows:
1. INTERPERSONAL RELATIONS
“for a gentle us”
2. COMPANY SITES / ENVIRONMENT
“for a friendly environment”
3. SELF-HELP
“for a gentle me”

Welfare Point

The Gentletude whiteboard It is an innovative tool which contains more features conceived and designed to make the enterprise, as well as a workplace, also a place that encourages people to common kindness and interpersonal relationships. Besides that on relations among employees, the board also acts on its places, making them home to meet and exchange. The tool allows for different activities and is divided into three main sections:
1. SHARED AGENDA
2. BANK OF KINDNESS
3. “KIND” EVENTS RECOMMENDED

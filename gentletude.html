GENTLETUDE IS WELLNESS

This is a teamworking thesis project designed for GENTLETUDE ONLUS. The world Gentletude imagines is a kind world to 360 ° in which people can share projects, spaces and common dreams. Our design for Gentletude ONLUS is started by asking a very important question: WHAT IS THE KINDNESS? During the first phase of the project we analyzed and discussed the concept of kindness, observing it in its many faces, in many environments and for different target. From there we started to draw a line on which we could build an intervention strategy. We set our goals, began the research phase and finally expressed our concept which is the basis of everything development work and its variations: kindness is wellness.

The project was declined by three major media / graphic tools:        1. Restayling
       2. Manual of Kindness
       3. Welfare Kit Gentletude

VALENTINO

YOUR PERSONAL SHOPPER

VALENTINO- Your Personal Shopper-is an academic project designed for the course of Digital Art Direction for Made in Italy and Luxury brands. The goal was to create a “new” product experience for an Italian luxury brand. My concept, designed for the well-known brand VALENTINO, is a personal shopper service and is born to offer customers a new digital luxury experience that is unique, professional and innovative. The exclusive service is to registered customers to which VALENTINO offers a fashion designers team that weekly creates tailored outfits with clothes bought in Online Boutique.

The client also in this section receives some advices from one of the designers to have a touch of style with the proposed outfits. Your Personal Shopper also offers weekly three new possible outfits with the clothing present in the Online Boutique, according to customer preferences and clothing purchased.

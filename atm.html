ATM MILANO

The ATM project is a work in teamworking made for the academic course of User Experience Design. Starting from a deep process of analysis on user needs and from an observation of their behaviours, we have created a web app for the company of transport ATM in Milan. Our design work is started by a first phase in which through Balsamiq program we could create interactive wireframes to perform the first phase of user testing for the new mental model proposed.

In a second step we took care of the layout design, It tries to express the brand identity of the company, but at the same time with a fresh and modern touch.
